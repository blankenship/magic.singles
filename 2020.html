All Your Walkers Are Belong to Us

B to the izz-O, L to the A-S. Welcome ladies and gentlemen to the 8th Wonder of the World (and if you get any of these jokes, we could probably be friends...)

It’s the year of our Elder Dragon 2020, and Nicol Bolas, Dragon-God is singleton and ready to mingleton. Old Granddaddy Grixis — you had me at “has all loyalty abilities of all other planeswalkers on the battlefield.” But wait! There’s more! Card draw and exile-flavored resource denial? Check. Abject creature/walker destruction? You bet. Weird ancient meditation mastermind alternate win cons? Bolobviously.

Like most of the War of the Spark Planeswalkers, sans his static ability he’s maybe a little underwhelming. And if you don't run into many Planeswalkers in your LGS meta or playgroup, there are likely better things Grixis can be doing with five mana (especially three black pips). But given the right meta, he’s Walker, Grixis Ranger taking over the game and giving your controlling heart what it wants — literally everything.

Floor:

A janky to cast Planewalker with a unique static ability that immediately comes down and is either a 5-mana kill spell or a zero-for-three in a 4-player pod. His -3 puts him in a vunerable 1 loyalty spot.

Ceiling:

A janky to cast Planewalker that immediately comes down and steals whatever the best Planewalker ability on the board is. The ceiling is whatever busted thing your Superfriends opponent is trying to do.

It’s Story Time

In a particularly-heated 4-player pod against a nasty Atraxa Superfriends deck, my opponent intentionally kept minusing their Tamiyo, Field Researcher just out of ultimate range to avoid me stealing the coveted “You may cast spells from your hand without paying their mana costs.” emblem — until I cast Volt Charge to kill another opponent’s creature and proliferated said Tamiyo to become the God-Pharoah of Omniscience Town. Still longing for the day I snag a Jace, Cunning Castaway clone ability and make Infinicol Bolases.

How I Use Bolas

Crosis’ Curses A Grixis control deck centered around passing out Aura Curses, denying resources, and swooping in with big nasty dragons.
