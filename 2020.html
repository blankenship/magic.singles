All Your Walkers Are Belong to Us

Nicol Bolas, Dragon-God is singleton and ready to mingleton. B to the izz-O, L to the A-S. Welcome ladies and gentlemen to the 8th Wonder of the World (and if you get this joke, we could probably be friends)...

Old Granddaddy Grixis — you had me at “has all loyalty abilities of all other planeswalkers on the battlefield.” But wait! There’s more! Card draw and exile-flavored resource denial? Big check. Abject creature/walker destruction? Bolobviously. Weird ancient mastermind alternate win cons? You betcha.

Like most of the War of the Spark Planeswalkers, without that static ability, he’s a little underwhelming. If you don't run into many Planeswalkers in your meta or playgroups, there are likely better things Grixis can be doing for 5-mana (especially coming up with those three black pips). But given the right meta context(s), he’s Walker, Grixis Ranger taking over the game and giving your controlling heart what it wants — everything.

Story Time

In a particularly-heated 4-player pod against a nasty Atraxa Superfriends deck, my opponent intentionally kept their Tamiyo, Field Researcher out of ultimate range to avoid me stealing the coveted “You may cast spells from your hand without paying their mana costs.” emblem — until I cast Volt Charge to proliferate their Tamiyo and emblemed all the way to the Omniscience bank.
